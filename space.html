Features

Space Client comes with all the features you would find in other clients, plus a few unique ones.

Spotify Integration

Lets you see what song you're playing currently, in game. No song controls available yet.

Custom Main Menu

A pretty clean main menu that lets you change your mod settings without going into a world.

Free Cosmetics

All cosmetics on Space Client are free and customizable.

Mod Menu

A simple to use mod menu to toggle your favorite mods on and off.
